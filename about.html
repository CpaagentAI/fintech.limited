About FinPro Directory

Revolutionizing financial services through AI-powered technology and professional expertise.

Our Story

FinPro Directory was founded with a simple yet ambitious vision: to transform the financial services industry through the power of artificial intelligence. We believe that every financial professional should have access to cutting-edge technology that enhances their capabilities and improves client outcomes.

Since our inception, we've been at the forefront of AI innovation in accounting, tax preparation, and financial consulting. Our platform connects certified professionals with advanced AI tools that automate routine tasks, improve accuracy, and provide deeper insights.

Based in Atlanta, Georgia, we serve clients across the United States, helping thousands of CPAs, accountants, and tax professionals streamline their operations and deliver exceptional service.

Patriot Tax Services Ltd

Our parent company, Patriot Tax Services Ltd, has been providing comprehensive tax and accounting services for over 15 years. We combine traditional expertise with modern AI technology to deliver unparalleled results.
